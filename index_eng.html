Card games

Card games are one of type of the board games, so simple and clear enough that most of them understand even small children. There are many types of card games, that's why everybody will find the favourite one.

Memory is a card game with the discovery of consecutive cards, the point is charged when both the cards are identical. When the cards are different, turn both cards back and the point is not counted. We have an example of memory game with actors. Try yourself now!

There is also something for more skilled players. There are more advanced card games, such as Solitaire or the Egyptian pyramid. Similar games can also be distinguished for more people, such as Poker, Bridge, Remik and many more!

Gwent - The Witcher Card Game

This Is a game developed by CD-Project on the need for the game "The Witcher 3-Wild Hunt". It Consists of arranging the cards selected and drawn from the waist on the battlefields. Each Card is a separate character in the Witcher series, has one of three classes, and the value of the attack force expressed in points. The Points on the battlefield are summed up, and the round wins the player who holds the most points until the opponent folds.

Monopoly is a classic real estate trading game. Invented in the United States during the Great Depression period by Elizabeth Magie. It Gives players the chance to turn big money and quickly enrich themselves based on The Landlord'S Game. Starting with the START field, you need to round the board, buying and selling real estate, building houses and hotels. For entering the property of other players pays rent. Success depends on relevant speculations, successful investments and wisely executed trades.
